Intelligence Group

Our group focuses on the premise of protecting a secret and futuristic facility located in a highly classified place only a few people know the true location of. This facility was designed by our engineers to serve as an advanced series of training methods in which there are various methods which overall aim to making of you the best agent possible. Through this you will encounter various branches and divisions in which each play a vital role in their respective fields and overall contribute to the facility personnel elite status.

Through your journey you will be tasked with attending trainings, preparing yourself against internal and external threats as well as dealing with all types of raiders and becoming the best agent we have to offer and therefore protect the facility.

To ensure this you will have to undergo, as stated before, various trainings in which aim training, reaction time, obby skills, guarding skills, communication abilities and loyalty will be key to complete and succeed in your entire formation.
